SnapRewrite Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026
Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Thank you for using SnapRewrite (the "App"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains how data is processed when you use the App.

1. Who We Are

App Name: SnapRewrite
Contact Email: pingldudu@gmail.com

2. What the App Does

SnapRewrite is a macOS menu bar writing assistant. Its core features include:

  1. Detecting and processing text you select in other apps.
  2. Sending selected text to your chosen AI model provider to generate rewritten output.
  3. Returning rewritten output for in-place replacement.
  4. Supporting custom commands, model/provider settings, writing style, and output language preferences.
  5. Supporting in-app purchase for one-time Pro unlock.

3. Data We Process

Based on the current implementation, the App may process:

  1. Selected text content, only when you trigger rewrite/command actions.
  2. Local app settings, such as writing style, output language, trigger mode, model/provider configuration, proxy settings, and custom commands.
  3. API keys stored locally for calling your configured AI provider.
  4. Purchase entitlement status (local + Apple) for Pro unlock state.
  5. Permission state (for example, Accessibility permission status) required for selection-based workflow.

4. How Data Is Used

Data is used only to:

  1. Provide rewriting, polishing, and translation functions.
  2. Save your local preferences for product experience.
  3. Validate and restore in-app purchase entitlement via Apple StoreKit.
  4. Maintain basic app functionality and troubleshooting.

5. Storage and Retention

  1. No account registration is required.
  2. We do not operate a developer-hosted cloud user content database for this App.
  3. Configuration data is primarily stored on your local device.
  4. Data sent to third-party AI providers is handled and retained according to their own policies.

6. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

When you trigger a rewrite action, selected text is transmitted to the AI provider you choose (for example, OpenAI-compatible endpoints, Zhipu, etc.) to complete the request.

We do not sell your text data.
However, your use of third-party AI providers is subject to those providers' own terms and privacy policies.

In-app purchases are provided by Apple. Payment information is processed by Apple, and we do not receive your full payment card details.

7. Permissions

The App may request or use:

  1. Accessibility permission, to detect text selection in other apps.
  2. Clipboard access in trigger scenarios, where text selection may be probed temporarily in some apps, with best effort to restore prior clipboard text.
  3. Network access, to send requests to your selected AI provider.

You can revoke these permissions in macOS system settings at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data without guardian consent, please contact us and we will handle it promptly.

9. Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  1. Modify or remove part of your local settings in the App.
  2. Remove local data by uninstalling the App (subject to macOS behavior).
  3. Contact us for privacy-related requests.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy due to product updates or legal requirements. The updated version becomes effective as of the date shown in the published version.
